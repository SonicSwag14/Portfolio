Commissions

Here's where you would put a official commission, as well as some examples and prices

Commission Details

Commission slots are available for personal and commercial use (depending on the project type). Each piece is created with care and attention to detail, following your references and descriptions. Once a request is submitted, you’ll receive confirmation, a price estimate, and updates throughout the process until completion.

Payment is accepted upfront or in two halves (50% deposit + 50% upon completion). Revisions are available at specific stages of progress (sketch, line art, color, etc.).

Commission Rules

  • No NSFW / 18+ content
  • No hate, discriminatory, or politically charged imagery
  • No gore or fetish material
  • Fanart and OC commissions are welcome (please provide clear references)
  • I retain the right to decline any commission that doesn’t align with my values or capabilities
  • Personal use only unless discussed otherwise (commercial rights are negotiable)
  • Credit is required when artwork is used publicly

How Commissions Work

  1. Submit a Request: Fill out the commission form or send a message with your idea, references, and desired category (Emote, Full Art, or Pixel Art).
  2. Review & Quote: You’ll receive a price quote and estimated completion time.
  3. Payment: Once approved, you’ll receive an invoice for payment (PayPal, Ko-fi, etc.).
  4. Progress Updates: Sketch previews and WIPs will be shared for feedback.
  5. Delivery: Final files are sent in high-quality format once completed and approved.
