Chronos Paradixium

Chronos Paradixium is a game that will be released in chapter parts over the course of a few years. This page is a detailed summary of the project.

Chronos Paradixium: Chapter 1

This game is in progress and is scheduled for release in 2026!

The game follows Aya and her adventure through time to retrieve her stolen research on time magic as well as the mysterious Chronosphere, a device capable of bending reality itself.

Chapter 1 – The Day Time Was Stolen

In Chapter 1, players step into Aya’s shoes on what begins as an ordinary research night in the city—until everything goes wrong. A shadowy thief breaks into her lab, steals her notes on experimental time magic, and escapes with the Chronosphere relic. What starts as a sudden rooftop encounter amidst the neon-lit skylines quickly spirals into disaster when the Chronosphere is activated by the thief, tearing open a rift in space-time.

Aya is hurled into a distant era she doesn’t recognize: a wild, untamed landscape of ancient ruins, overgrown forests, and unstable time fissures. Stranded far from home with only fragments of her research, she must learn to survive using elemental magic circles—intricate spell formulas she painstakingly designs herself. Players are introduced to the core gameplay loop here: exploring the environment for elemental resources, crafting magic to cast attacks, buffs, and debuffs, and experimenting with combinations to overcome threats.

As Aya delves deeper into a nearby cave system pulsing with distorted time energy, she discovers traces that the thief may also have passed through this era—and was possibly altering history to their advantage. Chapter 1 ends on a tense cliffhanger: Aya stabilizes her first Chronosphere pulse to glimpse another point in time, realizing that recovering her research isn’t just about getting her work back—it’s about preventing time itself from collapsing.
