Projects

These are the projects I've worked on. There are projects that were made in P5.js and RPG Maker as well as a few extra things from Twine.

Weather Channel

This is a short game experience made in Twine in 2024 about the randomness of weather. The player can choose what they want to bring in preperation for the weather, but you cannot predict the weather in the end so it's random. I had to make this for a class project, but I wanted to make something about how people can't predict things.

Naita Aka Oni Onnanoko

This is a short choice based game created in Inky in 2025 based on the Japanese folk story of "The Red Oni who Wept" also known as "Naita Aka Oni" in Japanese. It is an original choice based story that follows the original, yet puts new perspectives and choices from the perspective of the Oni's as two twin sisters Akari and Aoi.
