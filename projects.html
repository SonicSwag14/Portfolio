Projects

These are the projects I've worked on. There are projects that were made in P5.js and RPG Maker as well as a few extra things from Twine.

Dreamscape: A Visual Novel Experience

This project is a simple short visual novel made in P5.js as a project. I wanted to make a sureal experience in which the player goes through a looping and unknown scenario while trying to figure out what to do

Weather Channel

This is a short game experience made in Twine about the randomness of weather. The player can choose what they want to bring in preperation for the weather, but you cannot predict the weather in the end so it's random. I had to make this for a class project, but I wanted to make something about how people can't predict things.

Blacksmith Simulator

This project is a pixel blacksmith simulator made in P5.js. I wanted to make an interactive experience where people can just have fun making swords, while also having game aspects in which they can feel like they are achieving something.

  • Learn more

Naita Aka Oni Onnanoko

This is a short choice based game created in Inky based on the Japanese folk story of "The Red Oni who Wept" also known as "Naita Aka Oni" in Japanese. It is an original choice based story that follows the original, yet puts new perspectives and choices from the perspective of the Oni's as two twin sisters Akari and Aoi.

  • Learn More

Spore

This game was created in RPG Maker MV and was made to be a timed roguelike shooter rpg. Each time you die, you progress further into the level, allowing progression through experience.

Tanuki Trade

This game was created in RPG Maker MZ and was made to be a strategy puzzle game. It involves going around a nearby village to trade items with the locals until you reach a higher value than you started with before nightfall.

Year 500

This game was created in Unity, in conjunction with the group The Breakers Entertainment. I was in charge of enemy concept design, UI design, voice acting for the character Celeste, as well as some images from cutscenes.
